When I work for somebody, we actually work together

I don't care about my authorship much; I just try to help

I don't really follow any trends, more personal vibe

I give up perfection, let's enjoy the creative process

I don't support any form of oppression or user manipulation

I set the price based on the current situation of the person

Hello,

I am a [graphic] designer currently based in Berlin.

I am a part of Digital Public Services and a member of Loners.company. Besides that, I currently attend an internship with André Fincato.

In DPS, we currently research the general appearance of websites, collective rituals, and an avatar system based on weather forecasts (among others). My Bachelor thesis about Design System was also created as sa part of DPS.

With André, we research online calendars and create a digital tool for converting public events into calendar space.

I am always happy to get involved in interesting projects, especially when they support a local scene, art scene, or small businesses. I can help with designing digital platforms, websites, visual identities, type and printing material. I am currently learning to code but that will take some time. Feel free to sent me an email if you would like to cooperate!
